दिसपुर असमराज्यस्‍य राजधानी अस्‍ति । This place that existed as a locality on the outskirts of Guwahati, took its place as the capital of Assam in 1973. To the south of Dispur is the mythologically important site of Basistha Ashram and the Sankardeva Kalashetra, a cultural center created in the 1990s. Next to Dispur is the ancient township of Jatia.
