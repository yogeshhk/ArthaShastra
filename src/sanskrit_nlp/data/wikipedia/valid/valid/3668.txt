Odia आर्य-भाषा परिवारस्‍य एका भाषा. It is the official Language of the Indian state of Odisha
The Odia script, which like all Nagari writing systems is strictly speaking an abugida rather than an alphabet, is used to write the Odia language and somewhat resembles Devanagari and Thai scripts. Its numerical digits are different from their Devanagari counterparts.
